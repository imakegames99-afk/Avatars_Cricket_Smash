About This Website

Avatrs_Cricket_Smash is a platform created to provide high-quality 3D GLB models and textures for game developers, designers, and creators.

The assets are optimized for performance and compatible with popular tools such as Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender, and WebGL-based projects.

This website is fully static, fast, and secure. It does not require user accounts or collect data directly, making it simple and transparent for visitors.

The website is independently created and maintained by a developer with a passion for game development and 3D design.
